2. 不同的短期教学项目课时量有较大不同，且这些短期学习者有可能在将来转入长期教学班中学习，那么如何选择容量合适的短期教材呢？如何能与长期教材顺利衔接？

If you are a western learner, please note that this series doesn’t need you to master every Chinese character in the text. Hello Chinese is a full color course book with 4 main characters rendered with detailed backgrounds and distinct personalities. The stories in different lessons are somewhat inter-related.
