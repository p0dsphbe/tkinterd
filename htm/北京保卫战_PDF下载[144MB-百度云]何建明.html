作者简介

[

何建明，当代著名作家，苏州人。现任中国作协副主席，中国报告文学学会会长。三次中国文学zui高奖——“鲁迅文学奖”获得者；三次“徐迟报告文学奖”得主；四次中宣部“五个一工程奖”获得者；国家图书奖、中华优秀读物奖获得者。已出版文学著作近五十部，电影电视作品八部。 He Jianming, a well-known author of reportage literature, has been awarded the Lu Xun Literature Prize three times, the National Excellent Reportage Prize seven times, and the Xu Chi Reportage Prize four times. His main works include The Country, Fidelity and Betrayal, Fundamental Interests, and Falling Tears are Gold. He is additionally the author of the filmscripts Westbound Convict Train and Folksong Xintianyou, and the TV series Founders and National Action. He has published over 50 works of literature, including over 30 works of literary reportage, which have been translated into dozens of languages. He is vice president of the China Writers’ Association.
